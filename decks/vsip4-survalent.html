What is DERMS?

Distributed Energy Resource Management

The control layer that turns thousands of distributed assets — solar, batteries, controllers — into one coordinated, dispatchable resource.

Monitor

Real-time visibility of every DER's output, state, and impact on the grid.

Optimize

Decide the best action — curtail, charge, discharge — against grid limits and objectives.

Control

Send commands closed-loop, enforcing limits like zero-export automatically.

ADMS runs the wires. DERMS runs the energy resources on them. VSIP 4 needs both.
