Islanding & Black Start

When the national grid trips, the park keeps running

Restoration sequence

01

Each factory islands

Local microgrid controller + BESS forms an island.

02

Black start

BESS energizes the local bus → RTS reconnects → loads restored by priority.

03

Central coordination

DERMS stabilizes across islands for park-wide balance.
